MUTTON CURRY

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Mutton
  • 2Tbsp Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 2 Tsp Salt
  • 1 Tsp Turmeric Powder
  • ½ Cup Oil
  • 3 Bay leaves
  • 6 Green Cardamom
  • 6 Cloves
  • 2 Cinnamon
  • 8 medium sized sliced Onions
  • 2 sliced Tomatoes
  • 4 Tsp Red Chilli Powder
  • 4 Tsp Coriander Powder
  • ½ Tsp Garam Masala
  • 2 Green Chillies
  • ½ cup Dahi
  • 1 Cup Water
  • Few Coriander Leaves

    • method

    1. Marinate 1kg Mutton with Ginger Garlic Paste, Salt and Turmeric Powder. Cover with lid and keep it aside for half an hour.
    2. Heat ½ Cup oil in the pot of pressure cooker, put Bay leaves, Green Cardamom, Cloves, Cinnamon and let it cook for 1 minute.
    3. Add sliced onions and fry until turns golden in color.
    4. Add marinated mutton and saute it for 5 minutes on high flame.
    5. Add sliced tomatoes in the mixture. Close the pressure cooker with the lid and place the whistle on it. Cook on high flame until 1 whistle. Later let it cook on low flame for 10 minutes.
    6. After 10 minutes please check whether the mutton is cooked. Add Red Chilli powder, Coriander Powder and Garam Masala.
    7. Add Green Chillies and Curd (Please off the flame while mixing so that the curd does not curdle). Mix everything well.
    8. Now cook the Mutton on low flame and let it boil. Later increase the flame and roast it for 5 to 6 minutes so that all the spices are well cooked. Once the oil is separated from the mutton add 1 cup water and cook on high flame.
    9. Later close the pressure cooker with the lid and place the whistle on it. Cook it for 2 to 3 whistles.
    10. Garnish with Coriander Leaves and Its ready to serve with rice.
