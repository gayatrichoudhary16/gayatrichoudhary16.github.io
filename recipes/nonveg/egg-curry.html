EGG CURRY

  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 5 Eggs
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Chilli Powder
  • 1 Green Chillies
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder Haldi
  • Coriander
    1. In a pan heat up the olive oil on medium heat until hot.
    2. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook on medium heat for 5 minutes until softened.
    3. Add the salt, chilli powder, turmeric powder, and the chopped green chillies then cook the spices for 3-4 minutes.
    4. Add the eggs and stir continuously on medium heat for 4-5 minutes until the eggs are cooked through.
    5. Garnish with the chopped coriander.
    6. Serve alongside some homemade chapattis and enjoy!.
