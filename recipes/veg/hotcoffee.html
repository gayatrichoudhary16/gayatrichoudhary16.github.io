HOT COFFEE

Ingredient
  • Instant Coffee Powder - 1 tsp I use BRU
  • Sugar - to taste
  • Milk - 1 cup
  • Hot Water - ½ tsp

    • How to make hot coffee

    1. Boil some milk, keep it in simmering stage till you finish off the coffee mixture.
    2. Take coffee powder, sugar in a cup. Add ½ tsp of water to it and mix them well.
    3. Use a spoon or a small whisk and beat them like crazy. You will see the mixture gets foamy and thick and creamy.
    4. Once it reaches that stage you can add hot boiling milk to it and mix well.
    5. Serve immediately.
