MATKI SABJI

Ingredient
  • 100 gms mtaki
  • 1 onion medium sized
  • 2 Tomato medium sized
  • 1 inch ginger piece
  • 7-8 Garlic buds
  • 2 green chillies
  • 1/4 cup dry coconut powder
  • 1 tbsp coriander seeds
  • 1 tbsp groundnut
  • 3 pieces dry red chilli
  • 4 pinches hing
  • 1/2 tsp haldi powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp jeera
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • as required Coriander for garnishing
  • 4 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • 1 tsp jeera /cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp kashmiri red chilli powder

    • How to make matki sabji

    1. We soaked matki and made sprouts of it.
    2. These are all ingredients needed here.
    3. We dried roast grated coconut, peanut and khada dhaniya and raw red chilli.
    4. Add 4 tbsp oil in pan and add jeera and mustard seeds when it gets heated. Then add onion to it.
    5. Now add hing, Ginger garlic paste and salt when onion gets a bit brown in colour.
    6. Now add turmeric powder, red chilli powder and dhaniya powder.
    7. Now add tomatoes to it.
    8. When tomatoes start to lose water, add water to it and matki to it.
    9. Now add a little more water and cook by covering for 5-7 minutes.
    10. Now remove it in a bowl and add takda to it. So take a tadka pan and ghee to it. Add jeera and red chilli powder.
    11. Pour this immediately onto matki.
