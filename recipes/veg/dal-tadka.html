DAL TADKA

Ingredient
  • 1 cup washed & dried moong dal
  • 1/2 teaspoon asafoetida
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 2 diced onion
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 3 sliced & slit green chilli
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • salt as required
  • 1 red chilli
  • 4 cup water
  • 4 cloves chopped garlic
  • 5 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 stalk curry leaves
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 2 finely chopped tomato
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 1 teaspoon red chilli powder

    • How to make dal tadka

    1. Cook moong dal in pressure cooker To make this delicious Dal Tadka recipe, add moong dal in a pressure cooker. Add water and heat it over medium flame. Cook the dal for 2-3 whistles or until it gets quite mushy. After it's done, bring the flame to low and stir occasionally in between.
    2. Temper the spices in some oil Now add oil to a pan and heat it over medium flame. Once the oil is sufficiently hot, add mustard seeds and dried red chilli to the pan. Once the seeds start to splutter, add asafoetida, turmeric, red chilli powder, ginger paste, garlic, fenugreek seeds, cumin seed, curry leave and green chillies.
    3. Saute onion and tomato in the tempering Then add chopped onions. Once the onions turn slightly pinkish in hue, add the chopped tomatoes to the pan and fry them along with the mixture for around 5-6 minutes on low-medium. Once done, add the mushy dal to the mixture. Then add salt as per your taste and stir well.
    4. Add dal and simmer for 10 minutes Simmer the dal on low-medium flame for 10 minutes or until the tomatoes become tender. Keep stirring occasionally. After few minutes, switch off the flame and transfer the dal to a serving bowl. Dal Tadka is ready to be served. Pair it with rice or chapatis along with a salad of your choice. You can also serve it with some crisp potato bites and raita.
